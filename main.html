Summary

Based on a novella by Stephen King, *The Shawshank Redemption* is a timeless drama about hope, friendship, and perseverance. The film follows Andy Dufresne, a successful banker who is wrongly convicted of murdering his wife and her lover and is sentenced to two life terms at Shawshank State Penitentiary. Over nearly two decades, Andy uses his financial skills to find favor with the guards and warden. He endures brutality, builds a deep friendship with fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding, and quietly works towards an impossible escape, all while maintaining his sense of hope.
