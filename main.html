Summary

Inception is a science fiction film that follows a skilled thief, Dom Cobb, who has the unique ability to enter the dreams of others and steal their secrets. Tasked with planting an idea into the mind of a target, Cobb assembles a team to navigate the complex layers of dreams, facing challenges that blur the lines between reality and imagination.

Actors

The film features a talented cast, including Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Ellen Page as Ariadne, Tom Hardy as Eames, and Marion Cotillard as Mal. Each actor brings depth to their character, contributing to the film's intricate narrative.
