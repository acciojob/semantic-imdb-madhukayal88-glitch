Summary

The Matrix is a groundbreaking science fiction film that explores the nature of reality. It follows Neo, a computer hacker who discovers that the world he lives in is a simulated reality created by sentient machines. As he joins a group of rebels led by Morpheus and Trinity, Neo learns about his true potential and the fight against the machines that control humanity.

Actors

The film stars Keanu Reeves as Neo, Laurence Fishburne as Morpheus, Carrie-Anne Moss as Trinity, and Hugo Weaving as Agent Smith. Their performances are iconic and have left a lasting impact on the genre.
