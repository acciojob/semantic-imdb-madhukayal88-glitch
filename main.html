Summary

*Inception* is a mind-bending science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets by infiltrating dreams. Given a chance to erase his criminal record, Cobb must perform the impossible task of “inception” — planting an idea in someone's mind. As dreams within dreams unfold, the line between reality and illusion blurs.
