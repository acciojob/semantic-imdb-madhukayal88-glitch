Plot Summary

Inception is a mind-bending sci-fi thriller that follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in stealing secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. When offered a chance to have his criminal history erased, Cobb must perform the impossible: inception—planting an idea into someone's mind. As the team dives deeper into layered dreams, reality begins to blur.
